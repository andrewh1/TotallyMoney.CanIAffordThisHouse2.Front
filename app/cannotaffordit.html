How did we work this out?

  • House prices for any given postcode are based on the average for that area sourced from the Land Registry.
  • We assumed that the deposit required is 15% of the average house price in the given postcode.
  • House prices are assumed to change at the same annual rate as the average annual rate of change as measured over the past five years.
  • Annual house price change statistics are calculated by taking the average house price data from the Land Registry over the last five years and averaging the annual changes between them.
  • Savings that go toward the deposit are assumed to accrue no interest over time.
